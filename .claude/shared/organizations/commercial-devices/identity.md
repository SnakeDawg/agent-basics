# Commercial Devices Group — Commercial — Identity

> Who we are at the business unit level. The commercial-facing arm of Acme's PC, display, and peripheral business.

## Mission

Power the modern workforce with the most secure, manageable, and reliable client devices on the planet — from the everyday productivity user to the most demanding workstation professional.

## Vision

Be the indispensable end-user-compute partner for IT decision-makers — the vendor they call first when they think about devices, security, manageability, and lifecycle services.

## Position within Acme Computing

- One of two reporting segments (the other is IDG — Infrastructure Devices Group).
- **Commercial** sub-segment specifically: commercial PCs (laptops, desktops, mobile and tower workstations), displays, and peripherals — sold to businesses, public sector, and education. Distinct from CDG Consumer.
- The commercial portfolios in scope are **Acme Standard**, **Acme Plus**, and **Pro Workstation**. Portfolio-level positioning, SKU lineup, and brand definitions live in the portfolio docs — not here.

## Brand pillars

- **Engineered for IT.** Manageability, security, and lifecycle are first-class — not consumer features bolted on.
- **Performance for every workload.** From Acme Standard productivity laptops to Pro Workstation workstations doing simulation, the same commercial-grade engineering principles apply.
- **Services-attached.** AcmeCare, AcmeDeploy, and Acme-managed lifecycle reduce IT burden.
- **Sustainability-forward.** Recycled content, repairable designs, and packaging commitments embedded in commercial product roadmaps.

## What we are not

- A consumer-led brand (that's Acme Home).
- A pure-play workstation vendor (Pro Workstation and Acme Plus are two of the lines in the commercial portfolio).
- A peripheral-only vendor — devices are the anchor; peripherals attach.
