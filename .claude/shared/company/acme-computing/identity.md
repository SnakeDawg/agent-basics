# Acme Computing — Identity

> Who we are at the corporate level. This is the umbrella identity inherited by all business units.

## Mission

Help organizations and individuals build their digital future and transform how they work, live, and play.

## Vision

To be the most essential technology company for the AI era — from edge devices to cloud and on-premises infrastructure.

## Values

- **Customer at the center.** Decisions are anchored in customer outcomes, not internal preference.
- **Winning together.** One Acme — across hardware, software, services, and channels.
- **Innovation that matters.** Pragmatic invention focused on durable customer value.
- **Results.** Outcomes over activity; measurable impact over polish.
- **Integrity.** Do the right thing, even when no one is watching.

## Brand promise

Trusted technology partner across the full stack — from the desk to the data center to the cloud — delivered through industry-leading scale, services, and supply chain.

## Positioning anchors

- Largest commercial PC and infrastructure provider in the world.
- Single-throat-to-choke partner from device → infrastructure → services.
- Direct-plus-channel go-to-market model with global reach.

## What we are not

- A pure software or platform company.
- A consumer-only brand.
- A hyperscaler — we partner with and complement them.
