# Acme Computing — Strategy

> How we win at the corporate level. Inherited as constraint and direction by every business unit.

## Strategic vision (3-year)

Be the indispensable technology partner for organizations modernizing for AI: provide the broadest portfolio of devices, infrastructure, and services that meet customers wherever they are on the maturity curve.

## Where we play

- **Geographies:** Americas, EMEA, APJ — direct + channel coverage in 180+ countries.
- **Customer segments:** Large Enterprise, Mid-Market, Small Business, Public Sector (Federal, State/Local, Education), Consumer.
- **Categories:** Client devices (PCs, displays, peripherals), Servers, Storage, Networking, HCI, Data protection, Services (deploy, support, managed, financing).

## How we win

1. **Scale.** World's largest commercial PC vendor and #1 in external storage; supply chain and component access competitors cannot match.
2. **Portfolio breadth.** Only vendor with full-stack from desktop to data center to multicloud — enables single-vendor TCO conversations.
3. **AI infrastructure leadership.** Reference architectures, validated designs, and partnerships (NVIDIA, Intel, AMD) make Acme the default choice for enterprise AI buildout.
4. **Services + financing.** AcmeCare, AcmeDeploy, Acme Pay consumption models, and Acme Financial Services create commercial flexibility competitors must replicate.
5. **Direct relationship.** Direct sales for top accounts plus extensive channel — preserves margin while extending reach.

## Strategic priorities (next 12 months)

1. **AI revenue acceleration** — capture share of enterprise AI factory buildouts; expand AI PC category.
2. **Multicloud + edge expansion** — Acme Pay consumption models, edge platforms, telco modernization.
3. **Commercial PC refresh cycle** — capitalize on Windows 11 + AI PC upgrade window.
4. **Operating efficiency** — sustain margin through component-cost discipline and services attach.
5. **Sustainability commitments** — meet 2030 ESG goals (recycled content, packaging, renewable energy).

## Success metrics

- Revenue growth (segment-weighted), operating income margin, cash flow.
- AI server backlog conversion, AI PC category share.
- Net Promoter Score, AcmeCare attach rate.
- Channel partner satisfaction, direct customer renewal rate.

## Time horizons

- **Now (0–6 mo):** Execute the AI server backlog; defend commercial PC share against HP, Lenovo.
- **Next (6–18 mo):** AI PC category leadership; Acme Pay consumption growth; IDG–CDG cross-sell motions.
- **Future (18 mo+):** Edge AI deployment scale; sovereign AI infrastructure; new categories adjacent to client + server.

## Key risks

- Component supply volatility (memory, GPUs).
- Hyperscaler insourcing of infrastructure.
- AI PC adoption slower than category narrative suggests.
- Currency exposure outside North America.
