# Acme Computing — Charter

> Scope, authority, decision rights, and stakeholders at the corporate level.

## In scope

- All hardware, software, and services revenue and product lines worn under the Acme Computing umbrella.
- Two reportable segments: **Infrastructure Devices Group (IDG)** and **Commercial Devices Group (CDG)**.
- Global operations: supply chain, manufacturing, channel programs, services delivery.
- Brand, finance, legal, HR, IT, sustainability, and government affairs.

## Out of scope

- VMware (divested 2021; ongoing partnership only).
- Boomi, RSA, Secureworks (divested).
- Hyperscaler-style public cloud operation (we partner; we do not compete).

## Decision rights

| Decision | Owner |
| --- | --- |
| Corporate strategy, capital allocation | Office of the CEO + Board |
| Segment-level P&L | IDG President, CDG President |
| Brand standards, corporate communications | CMO |
| M&A, divestitures | CFO + Strategy + Board |
| Major customer escalations (top 100 accounts) | Account team + segment leadership |

## Key stakeholders

- **Customers:** Enterprises, mid-market, public sector, channel partners, end users.
- **Investors:** Public shareholders, debt holders.
- **Partners:** Microsoft, Intel, AMD, NVIDIA, VMware, Red Hat, hyperscalers, ISVs.
- **Channel:** Distributors, resellers, MSPs, system integrators.
- **Workforce:** Direct employees + extended workforce + manufacturing partners.
- **Communities:** Geographies where Acme has manufacturing or major employment.

## Operating cadence

- Quarterly earnings + segment reporting.
- Annual strategic plan refresh; mid-year update.
- Monthly operating reviews per segment.
- Weekly leadership team across IDG, CDG, services, and functions.

## Success metrics (corporate)

- Total revenue, operating income, free cash flow.
- Segment growth rates (IDG, CDG).
- Net Promoter Score, AcmeCare attach.
- Employee engagement, ESG scorecard.
- Total shareholder return relative to peer set.
