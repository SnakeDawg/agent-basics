# Pro Workstation — Portfolio Charter

> Scope, authority, decision rights, and stakeholders for the Pro Workstation workstation portfolio.

## In scope

- All Pro Workstation-branded mobile workstations (3000, 5000, 7000 series).
- All Pro Workstation-branded desktop workstations (compact, tower, rack form factors).
- Workstation-specific accessories where they materially differ from Acme Standard peripherals (e.g., specialty docks, ISV-certified displays paired with Pro Workstation).
- ISV certifications and partnership co-engineering for Pro Workstation SKUs.
- Pro Workstation-specific marketing, customer references, and analyst engagement.

## Out of scope

- **Acme Standard** (mainstream commercial laptops and desktops) — separate portfolio.
- **Acme Plus** — the other workstation-tier portfolio in CDG Commercial. Coordination required on positioning and SKU boundaries; separate ownership.
- **Acme Home / Acme Gaming** consumer-adjacent SKUs — Consumer organization owns those.
- **Server-tier workstations** beyond the Pro Workstation Rack form factor (true rack servers belong to IDG).
- **Application software** running on Pro Workstation — partner ISVs own that.

## Decision rights

| Decision | Owner |
| --- | --- |
| Pro Workstation portfolio P&L | Pro Workstation Portfolio GM |
| Roadmap (form factors, refresh cadence) | Portfolio GM + Engineering lead + Product Management |
| Pricing tier strategy | Portfolio GM + Commercial finance |
| ISV certification investment | Portfolio GM + ISV partnership team |
| Component partnerships (NVIDIA RTX SKUs, Intel/AMD platform choice) | Engineering + Portfolio GM |
| Major customer escalations | Account team + Portfolio GM |

## Key stakeholders

- **Customers:** Engineers, designers, scientists, content creators (end users) and the IT/procurement/operations roles that purchase for them.
- **ISV partners:** Autodesk, Dassault Systèmes, Siemens, PTC, Adobe, ANSYS, Bentley, Foundry, Avid, plus emerging ML/data-science tooling.
- **Silicon partners:** Intel (Core HX, Xeon W), AMD (Ryzen Threadripper Pro, Ryzen Pro), NVIDIA (RTX Ada Generation pro GPUs).
- **Channel:** Specialized workstation resellers, AEC/M&E/Engineering vertical integrators.
- **Internal:** CDG Commercial leadership, services org (AcmeCare Plus), supply chain (specialized component allocation), marketing (vertical campaigns).

## Operating cadence

- Monthly portfolio review (revenue, share, mix, attach).
- Quarterly roadmap review with engineering and ISV partners.
- Annual refresh launch synchronized with silicon partner platforms (Intel, AMD, NVIDIA refresh cycles).
- Continuous ISV certification testing as ISVs ship major releases.

## Success metrics (portfolio-level)

- Workstation revenue and unit volume by form factor.
- Workstation segment market share (overall + mobile-specific).
- Premium-tier SKU mix (Pro Workstation 7000 share).
- AcmeCare Plus attach rate.
- ISV certification breadth and "first-to-certify" wins on new platforms.
- Customer NPS within Pro Workstation buyer base.
