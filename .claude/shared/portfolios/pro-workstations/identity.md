# Pro Workstation — Portfolio Identity

> The ISV-certified workstation portfolio within CDG Commercial. One of two workstation-tier portfolios alongside Acme Plus.

## Mission

Deliver the highest-performance, most reliable, ISV-certified workstations for the world's most demanding professional workloads — from a CAD designer's daily driver to a render farm node.

## Vision

Be the workstation of choice for engineers, scientists, designers, and creators who treat their machine as a productivity multiplier, not a commodity.

## Position within CDG Commercial

- One of CDG Commercial's two **workstation-tier portfolios**, alongside Acme Plus. Sits above the Acme Standard mainstream commercial portfolio on capability, configurability, and ISV certification.
- The vehicle through which Acme competes against **HP Z** (Z2 Mini, ZBook Studio/Power, Z4/Z6/Z8 towers) and **Lenovo ThinkStation** (P-series + ThinkPad P-series mobile workstations).
- The bridge between client computing and adjacent IDG categories (e.g., AI workstations as a stepping stone to AI servers).

## Brand pillars

- **ISV-certified performance.** Validated and tuned for Autodesk, Dassault Systèmes (SolidWorks, CATIA), Siemens (NX, Solid Edge), PTC, Adobe, ANSYS, Bentley, Avid, Foundry, and other professional ISV stacks.
- **Reliability under load.** Engineered to run at full thermal and compute load for hours — not designed-for-bursts.
- **Configurability.** Towers and select mobile SKUs offer broad component choice (GPU, CPU, memory, storage, expansion) — the antithesis of sealed consumer designs.
- **Professional support.** AcmeCare Plus tier with dispatch SLAs designed for revenue-impacting downtime.

## What we are not

- A consumer or gaming brand (Acme Home and Acme Gaming fill those lanes).
- An ultraportable productivity laptop (Acme Standard covers that).
- A general-office desktop (Acme Standard covers that).
- A server (Pro Workstation is a workstation; the line stops at the workgroup, not the data center — though Pro Workstation Rack 7960 sits at the boundary).

## Form factors

- **Mobile workstations** — Pro Workstation 3000, 5000, 7000 series. Range from thin-and-light professional (5000) to mobile-replacement-of-a-desktop (7000 series with discrete pro GPUs).
- **Desktop workstations** — towers (3000, 5000, 7000 series), small form factor and compact desktops for engineers without floor space.
- **Rack workstations** — Pro Workstation 7960 Rack for centralized workstation deployment (CAD/CAE shops with thin clients, or media post-production).
