# Pro Workstation PC — Product Charter

> Scope, authority, decision rights, and stakeholders for the Pro Workstation PC product line specifically.

## In scope

- All mobile Pro Workstation SKUs (3000, 5000, 7000 series).
- All desktop Pro Workstation SKUs (Compact 3000, Tower 3000/5000/7000).
- Software stack shipped or recommended on Pro Workstation PCs: Acme Optimizer for Pro Workstation, Acme Display Manager, Acme Device Manager tooling specific to Pro Workstation deployments, ISV-certified driver packages.
- Customer-facing collateral: configurators, comparison tools, vertical solution guides.
- ISV co-engineering and validation work for Pro Workstation PC SKUs.
- AcmeCare Plus tier offers tied to Pro Workstation PC SLA needs.

## Out of scope

- **Pro Workstation Rack 7960** — covered by a separate rack-workstation product motion (different buyer, different deployment pattern).
- **Acme Standard** (mainstream commercial), **Acme Plus** (the other workstation-tier portfolio in CDG Commercial), **Acme Home** and **Acme Gaming** (consumer / gaming) — different products entirely.
- **Application software running on Pro Workstation PCs** — partner ISVs own that; we validate and certify but do not develop.
- **Server-class hardware** — that's IDG.
- **Component R&D** — Intel, AMD, NVIDIA own the silicon; we integrate.

## Decision rights

| Decision | Owner |
| --- | --- |
| Pro Workstation PC product roadmap (SKUs, refresh cadence, form factors) | Pro Workstation PC Product Director / GM |
| Hardware engineering (thermal, chassis, motherboard) | Engineering lead |
| Software stack (Acme Optimizer, drivers, validation) | Software PM lead |
| ISV certification scope and prioritization | Product Management + ISV partnership team |
| Pricing tier and configurator design | Product + Commercial finance |
| AcmeCare Plus offer design (Pro Workstation-specific) | Services team + Product Management |
| Vertical campaign prioritization | Marketing + Product Management |
| Major customer escalations (top accounts) | Account team + Product Director |

## Key stakeholders

### External

- **End users** — engineers, designers, scientists, content creators (the people who use the machine).
- **Buyers** — IT directors, procurement leads, design-team leaders (the people who choose and approve).
- **Partners** — Intel, AMD, NVIDIA, ISVs (Autodesk, Dassault, Siemens, Adobe, ANSYS, Bentley, Avid, Foundry).
- **Channel** — workstation-focused resellers and vertical integrators.
- **Analysts** — IDC, Gartner, Forrester (workstation tracker, AI PC commentary).

### Internal

- **Pro Workstation portfolio leadership** — sets bounds for Pro Workstation PC.
- **CDG Commercial leadership** — sets bounds for Pro Workstation portfolio.
- **Services** — AcmeCare Plus design and SLAs.
- **Sales** — direct + channel; product PM owns enablement materials.
- **Marketing** — vertical campaigns, analyst engagement, customer references.
- **Supply chain** — pro GPU and high-end component allocation.
- **Acme Financial Services (Acme Financial Services)** — leasing and Acme Pay PCaaS for Pro Workstation.

## Operating cadence

- Weekly product team standup.
- Monthly product business review (volume, revenue, share, mix, NPS).
- Quarterly QBR with Pro Workstation portfolio leadership.
- Quarterly ISV roadmap sync with key partners.
- Annual product roadmap planning aligned with silicon and ISV cycles.

## Success metrics (product-level)

- Pro Workstation PC unit volume and revenue, broken out by mobile vs desktop and by series (3000/5000/7000).
- Workstation segment share — overall, mobile-specific, tower-specific.
- ISV certification count (active + first-to-certify wins).
- Premium-tier mix (Pro Workstation 7000 as % of Pro Workstation PC revenue).
- AcmeCare Plus attach rate.
- NPS by segment (Engineering, AEC, M&E, AI/ML, Sci/Research, FinServ, Healthcare).
- Time-to-quote in channel (configurator complexity proxy).
