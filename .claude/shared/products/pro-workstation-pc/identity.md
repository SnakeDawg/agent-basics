# Pro Workstation PC — Product Identity

> The personal computer line within the Pro Workstation portfolio — mobile and desktop workstations sold individually rather than as rack-mounted infrastructure.

## Mission

Be the daily driver for professionals whose work depends on the responsiveness of their workstation — designers, engineers, scientists, and creators who would rather wait for nothing.

## Vision

The reference platform for AI-augmented professional work: a Pro Workstation PC should be the device that turns ML inference, photoreal rendering, and CAD simulation from "send to the render farm" into "wait a moment."

## Position within the Pro Workstation portfolio

- The mobile + desktop workstation line.
- **Excludes** rack workstations (Pro Workstation 7960 Rack), which target centralized deployment, render farms, and remote-graphics scenarios.
- Sits above the Acme Standard mainstream commercial portfolio (laptops and desktops) on capability, configurability, and ISV certification.
- Shares the workstation tier with Acme Plus. SKU-level positioning between Pro Workstation PC and Acme Plus is coordinated at the portfolio level.

## SKU lineup (current generation)

- **Pro Workstation Mobile 3000** — entry pro mobile; CAD reviewers, AEC field use, dev tools.
- **Pro Workstation Mobile 5000** — thin-and-light pro mobile; competes with HP ZBook Studio, MacBook Pro M-series, Lenovo ThinkPad P1.
- **Pro Workstation Mobile 7000** — desktop-replacement mobile; full-power Intel HX or Xeon, discrete pro GPUs up to RTX 5000 Ada.
- **Pro Workstation Compact 3000** — small-form-factor entry desktop.
- **Pro Workstation Tower 3000** — entry tower for general engineering use.
- **Pro Workstation Tower 5000** — mid-range tower with broader GPU and storage configurability.
- **Pro Workstation Tower 7000** — flagship tower; multi-GPU, dual-CPU options, server-grade memory.

## Brand pillars

- **Engineered for sustained load.** Thermal headroom, memory and storage capacity, and component selection tuned for production workloads, not consumer bursts.
- **Pro GPU first-class citizen.** NVIDIA RTX Ada (and successor) pro cards with ECC memory, pro drivers, ISV certifications across the lineup.
- **ISV-validated.** Tested and tuned for the dominant pro applications in CAD, CAE, AEC, M&E, and emerging ML toolchains.
- **Configurability where it matters.** Towers remain user-serviceable; mobile SKUs hit the right tradeoff between portability and performance for each tier.
- **Manageable at scale.** Inherits CDG Commercial's IT-friendly defaults — vPro, Acme Device Manager, SafeBIOS, SafeID — important when deployed in fleets.

## What we are not

- A consumer or gaming PC (Acme Home / Acme Gaming fill those lanes).
- A general-office laptop or desktop (Acme Standard covers that).
- A render-farm or HPC node (Pro Workstation Rack or IDG infrastructure).
- A budget option — entry-tier Pro Workstation is the floor; below that, Acme Standard.
