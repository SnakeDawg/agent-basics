# Pro Workstation Mobile 5000

The flagship thin-and-light pro mobile in the Pro Workstation line. Targets engineers and creators who need workstation-grade GPU horsepower without lugging a desktop replacement. Sits between the entry 3000 and the desktop-replacement 7000.

Wins on display quality and configurability. Loses on weight and battery life vs. ultraportable rivals — by design. The 5000 is a pro tool, not a productivity laptop.
