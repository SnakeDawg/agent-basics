# Pro Workstation Mobile 7000

The desktop-replacement mobile in the Pro Workstation line. For engineers who need the power of a tower but have to travel — or who simply prefer a single device that handles full-load CAD simulation and render work without compromise.

Heavier than the 5000 by ~1 kg and runs hot under sustained load. That's the trade for full-power Intel HX or Xeon-W with up to 192 GB ECC and dual-GPU options. Not a productivity laptop; a portable render node.
