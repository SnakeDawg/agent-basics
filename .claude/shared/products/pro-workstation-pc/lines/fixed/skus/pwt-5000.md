# Pro Workstation Tower 5000

The mid-range tower in the Pro Workstation line. Configurable from a 4070 Ada / 64 GB starter to a dual-RTX-6000 / 512 GB-ECC powerhouse without changing chassis. Targets the broad professional middle: not the flagship, but the one most pros actually buy.

Wins on configurability and ISV cert breadth. Loses on CPU ceiling vs. competitors who push higher-tier Xeon-W configurations into similar price bands.
